Admission Process & Details

Secure your seat at Forces Academy Faisalabad. Find all information regarding the application steps, eligibility criteria, required documentation, and fee structure below.

Section 1: How to Apply

  1. Visit Campus or Apply Online
    Obtain the official admission prospectus from the academy's information desk.
  2. Fill Application Form
    Complete the personal and academic details accurately on the form.
  3. Attach Required Documents
    Gather your photographs, previous board results, and CNIC/B-Form copies.
  4. Submit and Pay Fee
    Deposit the form along with the registration fee at the accounts office to lock your admission.

Section 3: Required Documents

  • 4 Attested Passport-size Photographs (Blue Background)
  • Attested Copy of Matric / 8th Class Result Card
  • Copy of Student's B-Form or Original CNIC
  • Copy of Father or Guardian's CNIC
  • Character Certificate from the previous school/college

Section 2: Eligibility Criteria

Matric Programs (Science / Arts)

Must have successfully cleared 8th Class examinations from a registered school or PEF pattern institution.

Intermediate Programs (FSc / ICS)

Minimum 60% marks required in Matric Science for Pre-Medical/Pre-Engineering, and 50% for Computer Science stream.

Entry Test Preparations (MDCAT / ECAT)

Open for students currently appearing in FSc Part-2 examinations or those who have already completed intermediate with relevant science subjects.

Section 4: Fee Structure

Course / Program Admission Fee (One-Time) Monthly Tuition Fee
Matric (Science / Arts) Rs. 3,000 Rs. 4,500
FSc Pre-Medical / Pre-Engineering Rs. 5,000 Rs. 7,000
ICS (Computer Science) Rs. 5,000 Rs. 6,500
Entry Test Preparation (Full Session) Rs. 2,000 Rs. 15,000 (Total Package)
